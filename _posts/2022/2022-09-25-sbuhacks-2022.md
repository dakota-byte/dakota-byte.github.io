### Our game

Potion Master is a memorization game where you play as the shop keeper behind a cart set in the medieval era. Customers approach you with an issue, and it's your job to sell them the right potion. Go quickly! Customers get impatient and a timer counts down to your doom.

### My role

I was the backend programmer of the project, meaning programmed the logic and majority of the game mechanics. My other two members focused on the front-end aspect, writing dialogue, and coming up with different types of potions.

### Our placement

We won Best Game award.
